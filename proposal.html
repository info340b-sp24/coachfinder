Porject Overview

Lots of people want to be more athletic, but often they don’t have any idea how to exercise in the gym. In this circumstance, they need to find a personal coach/trainer. However, it’s not easy to find a coach that fits a person well now, which leads to problems for those beginner-level fitness people.

Problem Description

Some gyms provide people with different coaches, but it’s often hard to use information technology to find one that fits a person well. This is one of the unaddressed information problems.

  • One example of a previous attempt to solve this problem in one form would be the app Exercise.com, which is primarily focused on allowing clients to make progress in the gym, rather than a specific sport. Excercise.com provides users with workout routines and the resources to track their progress, such as a notepad, calorie tracker, and a calendar. Although this can serve to be a very helpful tool in terms of tracking and giving clients a guideline for what to do, someone who wants to develop a more esoteric skill / has very specific goals would benefit more from an app that would allow them to find a coach that could satisfy their needs.
