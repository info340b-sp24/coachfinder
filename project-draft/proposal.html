Project Overview

Lots of people want to be more athletic, but often they don't have any idea how to exercise in the gym. In this circumstance, they need to find a personal coach/trainer. However, it's not easy to find a coach that fits a person well now, H which leads to problems for those beginner-level fitness people. Also, according to the latest stats, the number of personal trainers/coaches has increased in these years a lot, but the information is unknown to workout beginners.

Problem Description

Some gyms provide people with different coaches, but it's often hard to use information technology to find one that fits a person well. This is one of the unaddressed information problems.

One example of a previous attempt to solve this problem in one form would be the app Exercise.com, which is primarily focused on allowing clients to make progress in the gym, rather than a specific sport. Excercise.com provides users with workout routines and the resources to track their progress, such as a notepad, calorie tracker, and a calendar. Although this can serve to be a very helpful tool in terms of tracking and giving clients a guideline for what to do, someone who wants to develop a more esoteric skill / has very specific goals would benefit more from an app that would allow them to find a coach that could satisfy their needs.

App Description

Potential Users

People who do not have experience in gymnastics and are actively seeking coaches that fit them well. This app could provide various types of coaches that focus on different perspectives for users so that users can exercise effectively and improve their skills without many obstacles.

Functionality Walkthrough

We are expecting to provide several interactive features for users. The specification of those features and explanations are listed below.

  1. Create Profiles: Users can create profiles that they can access and use to navigate the app with a username and password. The profiles for Trainees will include
    • A profile picture
    • Age
    • Sport / event / athletic speciality they want to get better at
    • Availability
    The profiles for the coaches will include
    • A profile picture
    • Age
    • Phone number
    • Speciality
    • Years of experience in their speciality
    • The rate they will charge
    • Availability
  2. Filtering: The trainees are able to see the profile of coaches and are able to filter out their intended coaches. Possible filters including specialty, age, and charge
  3. Book appointments: After discussing with the coaches what time will work best, users can book appointments, which will show up in the “appointments” tab. These appointments will be tiny blurbs that include the date, time, location, and a small blurb the coach can write detailing what they will do in that day’s training session.
  4. Comment:Users can likewise comment on coaches, making them visible to a wider audience and helping them make decisions.

Information

Below are the information that users will be viewing in this app.

  • Coaches'profiles with their available time periods
  • Coaches'specialties
  • Cost and Pricing Structure
  • User reviews on those coaches
  • Discussion channel for users to chat with potential coaches
  • A place to leave feedback
  • User's appointment records
  • User's progress tracking

Conclusion

With this app, people have a place to find the coaches that they prefer and match them well. They do not have to worry that their lack of experience would negatively impact the effectiveness of the workout. In addition, users are able to see the feedback of coaches given by past app users. They get to see various information about the ability and personality of the coach from more perspectives. In this case, users will have a chance to enhance their workout skills with the help of coaches and see the progress much easier.
